Stakeholder Interview

Cristin O'Leary Jones

Executive Director at Project Giving Kids

Interview Insights

By interviewing Christin we were able to gain a better understanding of their goals as an organization.

  • "It would be interesting to track personal work hours in your website profile "

    Ideation: Gamification, Profile Section, Add Friends

  • "We want the website to be as easy as possible to use for parents and kids"

    Ideation: Focus on accessibility

Problem Statement

Parents with young and adolescent children struggle to find volunteering opportunities because the activities are not framed in a way that is engaging for children.

Solution

We believe that creating a website that appeals to children and encourages them to participate in their community will attract more visitors and increase user engagement.

Interview Insights
